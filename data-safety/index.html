Data Collected

Wage Tracker does not collect any personal data. All information entered into the app (work sessions, wages, earnings, notes, profiles, tax settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with any server or third party.

Data Stored on Device

Data Type Purpose Encrypted Deletable
Work session records Core app functionality – tracking hours and earnings Device-level encryption (Android) Yes – via app settings or uninstall
Wage & job profile settings Calculating session earnings Device-level encryption (Android) Yes – via app settings or uninstall
Tax profile settings Providing net pay estimations Device-level encryption (Android) Yes – via app settings or uninstall
App preferences Remembering user configuration (currency, rounding, etc.) Device-level encryption (Android) Yes – via app settings or uninstall

Permissions

Permission Why It's Required
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Keep the earnings timer running while the app is in the background
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM Start and stop scheduled sessions at precise times
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restore scheduled session alarms after device reboot
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Display ongoing tracking notification (Android 13+)
WAKE_LOCK Prevent the CPU from sleeping during active tracking

Deleting Your Data

You can delete all app data at any time by going to Android Settings → Apps → Wage Tracker → Storage → Clear Data, or by uninstalling the app. Both actions permanently remove all locally stored data.
