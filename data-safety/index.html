Data Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS. Stored data is encrypted at rest.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Wage Tracker is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Controls

  • You can export your data from within the app at any time.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data through the app settings.
  • You can contact us to request a copy of your data.
